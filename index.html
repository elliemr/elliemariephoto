About

How photography became a part of me.

  • Middle school years

    I remember the day I discovered that I had a passion and the eye for photography. We were cherry picking at an orchard when I stole my mom's phone and started to take pictures of everyone while they were picking. I remember thinking it was fun playing around with different angles and lightings. After that day I was determined to save my money for my own point and shoot camera.

  • Practicing portrait photography

    One day I found the family Costco camera that my mom would take to soccer games. I saw it in a new light. I brushed off the dust and started to learn camera settings through reading books and watching YouTube videos. I never had a knack for landscape photography so my early teen years were spent bribing my younger sister to be my models.

  • High school years

    Photography naturally became my job in high school as I continuted to practice and develop my skills. Little by little I took pictures of more people and recieved lots of referrals--word of mouth is everything!

  • Full blown photo biz

    Now I'm 21 and loving photography more than ever! I love all of the opportunities that photography gives me! Especially the opportunity to meet people like you and create human connections!
